ABOUT THE BAND

Jimmy Fitzsimmons is a Serbian garage rock band hailing from Novi Sad, born in 2024 out of a quest for authentic artistic expression. They blend chaos with killer grooves, broken rhythms, saturated guitars, and spring reverbs, creating a sound that boldly mixes funk, hip-hop, and rock. Their music is wild, loud, and unapologetically fun, designed to make you groove, laugh, and maybe even question your life choices. Welcome to the madness.

LATEST RELEASE: "TOO MUCH TOO SOON" (2024)

Their debut album, "Too Much Too Soon," is exactly what it sounds like: a glorious mess of raw energy and questionable decision-making. These guys practically trip over their own riffs, the arrangements are delightfully "loose," and yet, somehow, they've stumbled into something incredible. It's fun, it's short, it's stupid (in the best way possible), and it's packed with unforgettable grooves, killer guitar solos, and stories you won't forget. Featuring standout tracks like "Fake Tits," "Bronco Boy," and "This Ain't Fvcking Paris," the album showcases seasoned musicianship beneath its playful exterior. Welcome to the Jimmy Fitz Function!
